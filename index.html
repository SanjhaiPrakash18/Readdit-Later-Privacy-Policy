Introduction

Welcome to Readdit Later, a Chrome extension designed to help you save and manage your Reddit posts efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our extension.

Important: Readdit Later operates entirely within your browser and does not send your personal data to external servers unless explicitly stated. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Information We Collect

Information Collected Through Reddit OAuth

When you authenticate with Reddit using our extension, we collect:

  • Reddit Username: Used to identify your account and fetch your saved posts
  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens provided by Reddit for API access
  • Saved Posts Data: Content of your saved Reddit posts including titles, subreddits, URLs, scores, and metadata
  • Basic Profile Information: As permitted by Reddit's OAuth scopes (identity, read, history, save)

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • Your saved Reddit posts and their metadata
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Authentication tokens and session data
  • Synchronization timestamps
  • Usage statistics (posts saved, subreddits accessed)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Reddit password
  • Personal messages or private communications
  • Posts you haven't explicitly saved
  • Browsing history outside of Reddit
  • Personal identification information beyond your Reddit username

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  • Core Functionality: Display and manage your saved Reddit posts
  • Synchronization: Keep your saved posts up-to-date across browser sessions
  • Organization: Provide filtering, sorting, and search capabilities
  • Statistics: Show analytics about your saved posts and usage patterns

Data Processing Activities

  • Retrieving your saved posts from Reddit's API
  • Parsing and organizing post metadata
  • Creating local backups of your data
  • Generating usage statistics and insights
  • Facilitating post management actions (unsave, organize)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage APIs. This means:
  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly interact with Reddit
  • No third-party servers store your personal information
  • You maintain complete control over your data

Security Measures

  • OAuth 2.0: Secure authentication through Reddit's official OAuth system
  • HTTPS Only: All Reddit API communications use encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Token Security: Access tokens are securely stored and automatically refreshed
  • Rate Limiting: API requests are rate-limited to prevent abuse
  • Scope Limitation: We only request the minimum necessary Reddit permissions

Third-Party Services

Reddit API

Our extension communicates with Reddit's official API to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Retrieve your saved posts
  • Perform save/unsave operations
  • Access basic profile information

This communication is governed by Reddit's Privacy Policy and API Terms.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics services, tracking pixels, or advertising networks. Your usage of the extension is not monitored or reported to any external services.

Your Rights and Controls

Data Access and Portability

  • View Your Data: Access all stored data through the extension's detailed view
  • Export Data: All data is stored in standard formats and can be accessed through Chrome's developer tools
  • Data Portability: Your data remains in open, accessible formats

Data Deletion

  • Logout: Removes all authentication tokens and clears stored data
  • Uninstall Extension: Permanently removes all locally stored data
  • Clear Browser Data: Standard Chrome data clearing removes extension data
  • Selective Deletion: Remove individual posts or clear specific data categories

Reddit Account Controls

  • Revoke Access: Revoke extension permissions through Reddit's authorized applications
  • Account Deletion: Deleting your Reddit account invalidates all associated tokens

Data Retention

  • Saved Posts: Retained locally until you delete them or uninstall the extension
  • Authentication Tokens: Stored until logout or token expiration
  • Settings: Maintained until changed or extension removal
  • No Server Storage: We do not retain any data on external servers

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. Since the extension requires a Reddit account, users must meet Reddit's minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the extension's update notes
  • Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

International Users

This extension can be used globally, but please note:

  • Data processing occurs locally on your device
  • Reddit API communications are governed by Reddit's international policies
  • Your data protection rights depend on your local jurisdiction

Technical Information

Permissions Explained

Our extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: Save your data locally
  • tabs: Open Reddit links in new tabs
  • activeTab: Enhance Reddit browsing experience
  • alarms: Schedule background synchronization
  • identity: Handle Reddit OAuth authentication
  • sidePanel: Display extension interface

Host Permissions

  • https://www.reddit.com/*: Interact with Reddit website
  • https://oauth.reddit.com/*: Access Reddit's OAuth API
  • https://api.reddit.com/*: Access Reddit's data API
